Carl Manley & his music

What does Carl do musically?

Carl has been playing the piano sice he was six years old, moving onto guitar, drums and flute.



He also played in a samba (Brazilian percussion) band for 3 years and a steel pand band. Following this he took up music production and has been doing this for around 15 years now and loves it very much.
Although he has never released any of his music, he hopes to in the future. Nevertheless it is a life long hobby for him and something that he will always do.


Take a listen to his music!

You can follow this link to listen to a track on SoundCloud composed by Carl Manley called "Solacae", based on the soundtrack/filmscrore genre of music.
Carl is also making styles of music like techno, drum and bass, deep house and chillout electronica. He feels being so strict on the genre doesn't allow the creativity to flow naturally.
